About

A cleaner story, not just a longer publication list.

I am a postdoc at UvA and a member of the ELLIS program on learning with limited data. I work with Cees Snoek and have also collaborated closely with Xiantong Zhen. Before joining UvA, I completed my master's degree in Software Engineering at Beihang University.

I am especially interested in how models can generalize across tasks, domains, and label-scarce settings. A recurring theme in my work is learning better inductive structure through memory, prompts, variational objectives, or task-aware generation.
